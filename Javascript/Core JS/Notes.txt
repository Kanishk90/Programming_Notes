        Concept1: Operators in JavaScript
            Arithmetic Operator:--> +(add or Increment), -(sub or decrement),*(mul),/(divide)
                                    Other Operators: --> Modulus Operator(%), Exponentiation (**), Increment(++) and decrement(--)
                                    Unary Operators: -->post Increment(a++) pre Increment(++a)
            Assignment Operators: --> = , +=,-=,*=,%= **=
            Comparison operator:-->Equal to (==),Equal to & type(===),Not equal to(!=),Not equal to & type(!==),>=,>,<=,<
            Logical operator:-->Logical AND (&&),Logical OR (||),Logical NOT(!)

            Ternary operator:-->condition?true output:false output
                        ex:- age> 18?"adult":"not adult"
